Play as Boyfriend as you travel across interdimensional portals to different worlds of Indie Games! FNF: Indie Cross is a Friday Night Funkin' Mod/fangame created by Moro Nighteye and several other team members. The initial version of the mod was released on April 9th, 2022, featuring the likes of 'Cuphead', 'Undertale', and 'Bendy and the Ink Machine', in 3 main weeks. After stepping down as the lead, the project was directed by kaydotnet for some time, and today is currently in the hands of penkaru. The much-anticipated update is still being worked on, so stay tuned for more news in the future!
